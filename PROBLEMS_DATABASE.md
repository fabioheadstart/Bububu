# Bububu — Problems Database v1

> Format: Situation (2-3 lines) → 3 options (A/B/C) → Best answer + why
> CEFR: A1-A2 for Kids, A2-B1 for Adult
> Score: Solo correct = HIGH · Hint then correct = MEDIUM · Wrong = LOW

## 🧒 KIDS (A1–A2)

---

### K01 · Travel / Airport
**Situation:** You land at the airport with your family. Your suitcase doesn't arrive on the belt. Everyone else got theirs. Your parents don't speak much English.

- **A:** "Where is my bag? This is your fault!"
- **B:** "Excuse me. Our suitcase is missing."
- **C:** "Excuse me, sir. We checked one bag and it didn't arrive. Can you help us, please?"

✅ **Best: C** — polite, specific, asks for help clearly.

---

### K02 · Travel / Hotel
**Situation:** You check into the hotel. The room has only one bed, but your family needs two. The receptionist looks very busy.

- **A:** "This room is wrong. Fix it now."
- **B:** "We need two beds."
- **C:** "Sorry to bother you. We reserved a room with two beds. Is it possible to change?"

✅ **Best: C** — apologizes for the interruption, states the need clearly.

### A02 · Business / Unhappy client
**Situation:** A client sends you an angry email saying the work you delivered is not what they expected. They use strong language. You need to reply.

- **A:** "I'm sorry you feel that way, but the work meets the agreed specifications."
- **B:** "I'm very sorry. What can I do to fix this?"
- **C:** "Thank you for letting me know directly. I want to understand exactly what fell short. Can we schedule a call today so I can make this right?"

✅ **Best: C** — acknowledges, doesn't defend, proposes immediate action.

### A03 · Business / Asking for a raise
**Situation:** You've worked at the company for 2 years with strong results. You request a meeting with your manager to discuss your salary. You're sitting across from them now.

- **A:** "I need a raise. My friend at another company earns much more than me."
- **B:** "I'd like to discuss my compensation. I've added a lot of value here."
- **C:** "I've been tracking my contributions this year and I'd love to share them with you. I'd like to have a conversation about whether there's room to adjust my compensation."

✅ **Best: C** — evidence-based, professional, opens a conversation rather than making a demand.
